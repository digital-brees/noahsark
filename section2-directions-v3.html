Section 2 · Values — happy + depth

Editorial, but with joy and dimension

You liked the editorial mosaic; the client wants it to feel happy. The move: bright photography, periwinkle color pops, and sharp diagonal geometry for energy and depth — slants and overlap, not rounded blobs or waves (which would read "daycare"). Three takes below.

Direction G

Bento + periwinkle depth

A woven bento grid with mixed tile sizes. Each photo casts a periwinkle block behind it for depth and a hit of color, and one value lives on a bright periwinkle tile. Cheerful and dimensional without a single rounded gimmick.

Direction I

Diagonal-split duo tiles
