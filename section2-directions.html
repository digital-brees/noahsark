Section 2 · Values — three visual directions

Pick a direction for the Values block

Same four values, three ways to make the section earn a pause. Photos are existing stand-ins — real candids would swap in. All three stay inside the locked system (navy / paper / periwinkle, Bricolage + Inter, hairlines, small radii, solid scrims — no gradients). Tell me A, B, or C (or a mix) and I'll build it into the live team page.

Direction A

Image-anchored split

A tall, warm photo anchors the left; the four values read as a refined two-column list beside it. Editorial and calm — the image does the stopping, the hairline grid keeps it sophisticated. A small floating caption adds a human note.

Direction B

Cinematic full-bleed band

A full-width photo with a deep navy scrim turns the section into an immersive moment. Headline up top, the four values as overlaid white columns at the bottom. This is the strongest "stop and look" — it breaks the white rhythm of the page with a cinematic dark band.
