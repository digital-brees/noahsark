Does Noah's Ark offer 24-hour emergency care?

No. Noah's Ark handles urgent and emergency cases during our regular business hours, Monday through Saturday. We do not provide overnight or 24-hour emergency service.

For emergencies outside our hours, please reach a dedicated 24-hour emergency hospital. When in doubt, it's always safer to call.

What counts as a pet emergency?

Call right away for trouble breathing, collapse or fainting, suspected poisoning, severe bleeding, repeated vomiting or diarrhea, a swollen or painful belly, inability to urinate, seizures, or any sudden severe pain. In cats, sudden paralysis or severe pain in the hind legs is an emergency.

When you're unsure whether something is serious, call us and we'll help you decide.

How can I prevent emergencies in the first place?

Many urgent problems are caught earlier through routine care. Keeping up with wellness exams, parasite prevention, and recommended lab work helps us find issues before they become emergencies.

If something seems off with your pet, it's always worth a call sooner rather than later.
