Section 2 · Values — interactive

Three ways to make it move

Working prototypes — hover or click to feel them. Each keeps the editorial mosaic energy plus the periwinkle joy and sharp geometry, and adds real interaction. All are keyboard-operable and fall back gracefully with reduced-motion on. The note next to each tells you what to try.

Direction JHover / click a panel

Expanding image panels

Four photo panels share the row; hover or click one and it expands to reveal the value, the others slide aside. Image-forward, alive, and the slanted periwinkle number badge keeps it cheerful. This is the classic "stop and play" interaction.

Reveal-on-hover mosaic

The editorial mosaic you liked — but each tile teases the value name, then slides a navy panel up to reveal the full line on hover. Bento sizing gives depth; the reveal gives life. Tap works on touch.

Direction LHover / click a value

Interactive showcase

The four values become a menu; pick one and a big image crossfades in with its line. Editorial and calm, but interactive — and the large photo stage is the "stop and look." Great if you want one hero-quality image at a time.
